Welcome to Blood Donors List App





About this Website


This website lists the names of volunteers who are willing to donate blood. Users who would like to enrol as a donor can register by clicking the register button and filling the form that follows. There is a need to collect and store the details of verified blood donors, so that they are easily accessible when needed.

Services provided by the Website


  • View verified Blood Donors
  • Enroll as a Blood Donor
  • Edit personal data
  • Remove yourselves from Donor List
